Middy's

Data & Electrical Wholesalers

Middy’s is Australia’s largest data and electrical wholesaler, with over 100 branches nationwide. While at Tangent Design, I collaborated with the Middy’s Marketing department to deliver a wide range of digital and print assets supporting promotional campaigns, event marketing and branch communications - all in Middy’s trademark pink.

I led the rollout of all campaign assets - including printed collateral, EDMs, web graphics, in-branch assets and Meta socials. For events such as Info Nights, I managed the entire rollout schedule, coordinating with Middy’s event coordinator to deliver each asset on time. Working with Middy’s has allowed me to combine my skills in print, digital design, email marketing and motion graphics to help strengthen Middy’s brand presence across multiple channels.
