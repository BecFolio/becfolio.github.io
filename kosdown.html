Kosdown

Melbourne's Leading Printers

While at Tangent Design, I delivered content to promote Kosdown’s services across print and digital platforms including brochures, email campaigns, website content and social media. I also designed client promotional material including calendars, deskpads and wall planners. Everything was designed to not only look great but to highlight Kosdown’s own print capabilities, reinforcing the brand every day on people’s desks and walls.

For 5 years, I also led the design and rollout of Kosdown’s annual Footy Tipping campaign—shaping the look, feel and execution across socials, EDMs and campaign assets that kept the tipping fun and on brand whilst coordinating with the third-party tipping platform (I even joined in on tipping fun but sadly Dees can’t kick goals).
