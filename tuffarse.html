Tuff Arse Camp Chairs

Camp Chair Brand

While at Tangent Design, I was involved in shaping the Tuff Arse brand alongside the founder and creative director. This was a dream project where I helped build the brand from the ground up, injecting personality through idea generation, illustrations, witty copywriting, videos, social media content, print, and email marketing to connect with an Aussie audience. All characters and illustrations were created by me — I came up with the ideas, drew them, and brought them to life with playful animations and videos.

I also concepted, designed, and animated a full set of cheeky, on-brand Shopify emails for Tuff Arse — from order placed to abandoned cart — all featuring my animated illustrations and oozing with tuff personality. My goal was to make every step feel fun rather than transactional and to give the user a laugh along the way.
