Thorburn Property

Commercial Real Estate Agency

During my time at Tangent, I was responsible for revitalising Thorburn Property’s visual identity to elevate its professional image and ensure the brand stood out in the competitive commercial real estate sector. The project began with a refreshed company logo and expanded into the creation of a comprehensive brand and style guide. The new branding was applied across both print and digital touchpoints, including stationery, presentation materials, signage, EDM marketing, and real estate platforms. I also collaborated closely with the web developer to implement the updated brand on the company’s website.
