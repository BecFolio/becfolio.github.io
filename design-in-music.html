Design in Music

Publication

This book was a personal project hand-assembled to show how music and design intersect and have a lot in common in terms of creativity. I wanted to highlight how they cross paths in a number of different mediums including album covers, posters, logos, music videos and fictional bands. ‘Design in Music’ features photography in laneways of Melbourne and a deep investigation into the impact design has in the music culture including artists such as Storm Thorgerson, Jason Munn and The Gorillaz. The cover of the book is a play on a record cover sleeve again highlighting how music and design can intersect.
