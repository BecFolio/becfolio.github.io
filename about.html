About Me

Senior Brand Designer | Multi-disciplinary across brand, digital, print & motion

I’m a multi-disciplinary designer based in Melbourne, with over 10 years’ industry experience. I have strong all-round skill set across brand rollout, visual identity, UI design, animation, illustration and a passion for bringing ideas to life through hands-on work in the Adobe Creative Suite.

Whilst working in a design studio, i’ve enjoyed collaborating with workmates and directly with clients to deliver creative solutions across multiple platforms. I’ve helped roll out brands across multiple applications, developing visual identities and crafting engaging campaigns. I particularly like brand rollouts and injecting personality through tone, illustration and motion.

I’m always looking to evolve my skills and embrace new technologies - lately I’ve been growing my motion design skills and exploring new tools like AI to stay current and push my work further.
